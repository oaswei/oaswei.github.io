Skateistan

Although it started in Afghanistan, Skateistan now runs in Cambodia and South Africa too, reaching thousands of children and youth. What started with a board and four wheels is now an award-winning, international NGO (Non-Governmental Organization) with the mission of empowering children and youth through skateboarding and education. Almost 10 years on, Skateistan is creating leaders that make a better world.

Currency Calculator

The following currency calculator will provide you with two helpful bits of information prior to your purchase.

1. It will inform you of the approximate cost of your purchase in U.S. Dollars based upon today's exchange rates.

2. It will let you know how today's exchange rates compare to a 50-day moving average. If you are above the 50-day average, it may be advantageous to wait a week or two prior to purchasing. Conversely, if current rates are below their 50-day average, you may want to take advantage of the exchange rates while they are in your favor.
